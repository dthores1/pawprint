# Whiskerville

## Animal Rescue Management Software

Whiskerville is a modern web application designed to help animal rescue organizations manage their daily operations more efficiently.

Animal rescues and foster-based organizations often rely on spreadsheets, paper records, text messages, and multiple disconnected tools to track animals, fosters, adoptions, medical care, volunteers, and supplies. Whiskerville brings these workflows together in a single platform.

## Features

### Animal Management

Track animals throughout their rescue journey, including intake information, medical history, photos, notes, and adoption status.

### Foster Management

Manage foster placements, foster applications, and foster availability while maintaining a complete history of each animal's placements.

### Medical Records

Record vaccinations, spay/neuter procedures, medications, clinic visits, and other important medical information.

### Adoptions

Track adoption applications, approved adopters, adoption outcomes, and post-adoption records.

### Volunteer & Organization Collaboration

Invite team members, assign responsibilities, and securely share information across your rescue organization.

### Supplies & Operational Support

Manage supply requests, transportation requests, and other operational workflows that help rescues care for animals efficiently.

## Our Mission

Whiskerville exists to help animal rescue organizations spend less time managing spreadsheets and paperwork and more time helping animals find safe homes.

By improving organization, communication, and record keeping, we hope to support rescues, fosters, volunteers, and adopters in their mission to save lives.
